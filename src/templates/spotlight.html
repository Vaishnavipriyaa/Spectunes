Justin Bieber


Justin Bieber is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and actor. He gained fame in his early teenage years with songs like "Baby", "One Time", and "One Less Lonely Girl". He has since released several successful albums and singles, including "Sorry", "Love Yourself", and "Intentions". Bieber has won numerous awards, including a Grammy Award, 20 Billboard Music Awards, and 20 MTV Europe Music Awards.
